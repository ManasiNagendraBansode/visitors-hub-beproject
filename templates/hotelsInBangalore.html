About WelcomHotel Bengaluru

Offering an outdoor pool, a fitness centre and a spa and wellness centre, WelcomHotel Bengaluru is located in Bangalore. It operates a 24-hour front desk to welcome guests at all hours. The hotel also offers happy hours at the bar lounge Swizzle.
Each room here will provide you with a TV, air conditioning and a minibar. There is also an electric kettle. Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and bathrobes. You can enjoy garden view from the room. Extras include a seating area and satellite channels.
At WelcomHotel Bengaluru you will find a restaurant and a fitness centre. Other facilities offered at the property include meeting facilities, a shared lounge and a ticket service. The property offers free parking.

About The Leela Palace

Located in Kolkata’s main shopping district, the New Market and business district, The Oberoi Grand Kolkata houses an outdoor pool, gym and spa.
The hotel is 3 km from Sealdah Railway Station and 6 km from Howrah Railway Station. Kolkata Airport is a 45-minute drive away. Offering pool and courtyard views, rooms at The Oberoi Grand feature a 32-inch flat-screen TV and a DVD player. A personal safe and a minibar are included. Private bathrooms come with Ayurvedic toiletries.
Baan Thai offers Thai specialities. Alternatively, the pool bar offers light meals and refreshing drinks. Threesixtythree° offers European, Asian, Japanese and Indian delights.

About Conrad Bengaluru

Ideally located 500 m from MG Road, Conrad Bengaluru is located in the heart of the central business district. With five distinct dining venues, 24-hour fitness centre and an infinity pool, each room and suite overlooks the city skyline and Ulsoor Lake.The hotel features a hot tub and a 24-hour front desk.
At the hotel, every room is equipped with a desk. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning and a TV, and selected rooms also feature a city view. The units at Conrad Bengaluru are equipped with a private bathroom fitted with a bath or shower.
Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a buffet or a gluten-free breakfast. At Conrad Bengaluru you will find a restaurant serving European cuisine. At the hotel guests are welcome to take advantage of a sauna.
Commercial Hubs in close proximity include RMZ Millenia 950 meters, RMZ Infinity, Old Madras Road 5 km away, Bagmane Techpark, CV Raman Nagar within 5.7 kilometers, Embassy Golf Links, Old Airport Road 6 km and UB City 4 km from the property. Brigade Road is 1.8 km from Conrad Bengaluru. The nearest airport is Kempegowda International Airport, 38 km from the accommodation.

About ITC Windsor

Set just 3 km away from the business district, ITC Windsor Bengaluru offers a spacious outdoor pool, a gym with 24-hour room service, 4 resturants and 1 BAR. Elegantly furnished rooms are fitted with free Wi-Fi.
Conveniently placed 33 km away from Bengaluru International Airport, the hotel is also 18 km away from Bangalore International Exhibition Centre and 2 km from Bangalore Palace.
Air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a sofa seating area, a cable TV or flat-screen TV, a minibar and tea/coffee making facilities. En suite bathrooms include a bath, hot/cold shower facilities and free toiletries.
Guests can enjoy a range of spa treatments while a 24-hour front desk can assist with luggage storage travel arrangements and round the clock in-room dining. Personal butler services are also available upon request.
